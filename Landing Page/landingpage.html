Tour & Travels

The city is dotted with magnificent temples, most of them dedicated to Lord Krishna,the city’s presiding deity and its first citizen. These temples depict various phases of Lord Krishna’s life, taking the devotees back to the divine era. Mathura is an important pilgrimage destination of the Hindus and is one of the seven sacred cities of India.
